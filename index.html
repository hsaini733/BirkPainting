About Birk Painting

Birk Painting was founded in 2001 and has painted over 1000 projects to date. We pride ourselves on being professional, on-time, on budget, clean and trustworthy. We are a team of professionals who guarantee your job is done right, on time and on budget. We focus on quality, efficiency & above all customer satisfaction. We protect & cover floors, furniture & any surfaces that are not to come in contact with paint.
